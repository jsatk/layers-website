The
Deets

Layers is a 3-day design conference taking place in San Francisco June 13—15 at the same time as WWDC. It’s an opportunity to come together in downtown San Francisco during the biggest Apple developer event, experience meaningful and entertaining content dedicated to design, and meet industry peers.

Passes to the conference are $850 (transferable but non-refundable) which will allow you access to all conference events. Transportation, lodging, and meals must be provided seperately.

Just 2 blocks from WWDC, we're just steps away from cool hotels, awesome resturants, and top-notch nightlife.
