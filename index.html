The
Deets

Layers is a 3-day design conference taking place in San Francisco June 13—15 at the same time as WWDC. It’s an opportunity to come together in downtown San Francisco during the biggest Apple developer event, experience meaningful and entertaining content dedicated to design, and meet industry peers.

Passes to the conference are $850 (transferable but non-refundable) which will allow you access to all conference events. Transportation, lodging, and meals must be provided seperately.

Just 2 blocks from WWDC, we're just steps away from cool hotels, awesome resturants, and top-notch nightlife.

Bespoke @ Westfield San Francisco Centre

Jessie Char

Jessie is a former cellst who left the glamorous classical music industry for the even more glamorous Mac development and design industry. She's put in her time behind [genius] bars, had an infamous stint at Delicious Monster, a more regular stint at Mule Design, and co-founded and headed the design agency Pacific Helm. She now works as an independent consultant directing design and producing very ambitious conferences.

Elaine Pow

Elaine is a design enthusiast whose retail and marketing career has been all about making you want to buy things. She’s worked for a handufl of retailers you’ve probably visited at the mall and at One Infinite Loop she defined global visual merchandising standards (made things look great) for Apple retail and the in-store iPhone launhc. These days Elaine walks the line between optimist and realist as a retail and brand development consultant.
